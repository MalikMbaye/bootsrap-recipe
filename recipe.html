French Toast That’ll Make You Boast

By Mālik Mbaye

I’m a simple man. I don’t need much. So typically, I have no issue bucking breakfast. That could also be due to the fact that I can’t cook. But when I’m feeling especially inspired, I’ll make some French Toast.
Ingredients
  • 8 Slices of Texas Toast (but if you’re like me, any kind of bread will do
  • 1/4 teaspoons of ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 4 tablespoons of Butter
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/4 Cup of Milk
  • 1/2 teaspoons of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine, cinnamon, nutmeg, and sugar and set aside briefly.
  2. In a 10-inch skillet, melt butter over medium heat.
  3. Whisk together cinnamon mixture, eggs, milk, and vanilla. Pour into shallow container such as pie plate.
  4. Dip bread in egg mixture.
  5. Fry slices until golden brown, then flip to cook the other side.
  6. Serve with syrup, fruit, or your choice of sides.
